ShiftIt

Managing window size and position in OSX

ShiftIt is a small, nimble utility that allows you to quickly organize and full-screen your Mac OSX windows.

Simply press ⌃⌥⌘ and the arrow keys to quickly send a window to take up the whole half of your monitor.

Or press ⌃⌥⌘+F to maximize it and take up the full screen faster than you can even think about pressing that unreliable green circle.

ShiftIt simply makes managing windows fast and easy.

Download

Download ShiftIt for OSX 10.7 and Up

Unfortunately builds for older versions of OSX are not currently available. ShiftIt is Open Source and easy to build, so you may be able to build your own! See more at the GitHub Project Page
